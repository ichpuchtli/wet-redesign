Harvesting and Storage

An integral part of a sustainable irrigation system is the harvesting and storage of its water supply. Water Equipment Technology is an international designing and consultancy company that specialises in designing harvesting and storage systems of irrigation water. For those looking to start a new harvesting or storage facility, WET can help by:

  • Sizing Storage Based on Harvesting Opportunities and Irrigation Requirements.
  • Surveys and Location Selection
  • Soil Testing and Soil Treatment
  • Dam Liners
  • Engineered Referable Dam Wall Designs & Specification
  • Pumping and Filtration Systems
  • Evaporation & Seepage Measuring & Monitoring
  • Anti-evaporation Techniques & Products

For those who already have such a facility, WET can help support or modify existing infrastructure through:

  • Below & Above Ground Tank Selection & Specification
  • Engineered designs & specification
  • Gross Pollutant Trap design & specification
  • Pumping and Filtration Design & Specifications
  • Automatic Monitoring of Tank Water Levels
  • Automatic Transfer of Water between Tanks
