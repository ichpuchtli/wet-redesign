Lakes & Ponds

Water Equipment Technology utilizes the latest in aeration and nutrient dissolution to create lakes and ponds suitable for any need, such as effluent lagoons and industrial water storage.

Aeration

Dissolved oxygen in the water creates a platform of aerobic digestion and aquatic animal life support. Bacteria which digest nutrients require oxygen to survive. This is a range of aeration products that offer aesthetic appeal while adding oxygen and breaking stratification.

Water Nutrition

Keeping nutrients at a minimal level is a prime requirement in an eco-balanced water body. Aerobic digestion is a key element in the reduction of nutrients and keeping the water clean and healthy.
